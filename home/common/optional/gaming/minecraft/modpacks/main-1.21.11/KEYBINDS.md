# Keybind Overrides

Most overrides are in `options.txt` (Minecraft keybind format).
Mods with their own config files note the path below.

## Vanilla

- `loadToolbarActivator` unbound — conflicts with hotbar 6 (X)

## Iris Shaders

- `iris.keybind.reload` unbound — conflicts with hotbar 9 (R)
- `iris.keybind.shaderPackSelection` unbound — conflicts with InventoryHUD config (O)

## Xaero's Maps

- `xaero_open_map` rebound to J
- `xaero_toggle_minimap` bound to F9

## ReplayMod

- `replaymod.marker` unbound — conflicts with Litematica main menu (M)

## Simple Voice Chat

- `mute_microphone` unbound — conflicts with Litematica main menu (M)
- `hide_icons` unbound — conflicts with MiniHUD toggle (H)

## Redstone Multimeter

Config: `config/redstone_multimeter/hotkeys.txt`
> Note: Both `options.txt` and `hotkeys.txt` must be updated together or the mod resets the keybind on launch.

- `toggleMeter` rebound to \ — conflicts with Litematica main menu (M)
- `resetMeter` unbound — conflicts with Xaero's Waypoints menu (B)
- `toggleHud` unbound — conflicts with MiniHUD toggle (H)
- `toggleFocusMode` unbound — conflicts with hotbar 5 (F)
- `pauseTimeline` rebound to Up arrow — was N
- `stepBackwardTimeline` rebound to Left arrow — was ,
- `stepForwardTimeline` rebound to Right arrow — was .
- `toggleTickMarker` rebound to Down arrow — was Y
- `openMultimeterScreen` rebound to F8 — conflicts with drop (G)

## G4meSpeed

Config: `g4mespeed/cache/hotkeys.cfg`

- `tps:halve` rebound to ; — conflicts with Xaero's map (J)
- `tps:double` rebound to ' — conflicts with Iris toggle shaders (K)
- `gs:opengui` unbound — conflicts with drop (G)

## Elytra Pitch

- `Toggle Flight HUD` unbound — conflicts with MiniHUD toggle (H)

## InventoryHUD

- `inventoryhud.toggle` unbound — conflicts with Litematica info overlay (I)

## Technical Utilities

Config: `config/techutils.json`

- `rotatePlacement` unbound — conflicts with hotbar 9 (R)
- `compactScoreboard` rebound to F7 — conflicts with Redstone Multimeter screen (F6)
